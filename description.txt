Create a portal from Carbon-Steel blocks (default) and put down a DHD near it to register that portal.

~bbbbb~
bb___bb				b = block (default carbon-steel)
b_____b				_ = air
b_____b				~ can be anything
b_____b				
bb___bb
~bbbbb~

Portals teleport anybody (and any object such as vehicles or mobs) nearby (1block) to the destination portal
